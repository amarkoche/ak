Free Website Design & Development by akSoftTech

Why is akSoftTech providing free websites?

akSoftTech aims to help small and new businesses establish their online presence. That’s why we offer basic website design and development completely free.

Do I really not have to pay anything for the website?

Yes! We provide website design and development for free. You only need to pay for the domain and hosting (if required).

Are there any hidden charges for the free website?

No, there are no hidden charges. The basic website is 100% free. However, if you need additional services like SEO, digital marketing, or advanced customization, those will be paid services.

What will I get with the free website?

  • A professional & responsive website design
  • Basic development and setup
  • Mobile-friendly interface
  • User-friendly layout

Can I update my website after it's created?

Yes! We will provide basic training if you want to update the website yourself. If you want us to manage updates, it will be a paid service.

Will you provide domain and hosting for free?

No, domain and hosting costs need to be paid by the client. However, we can suggest the best and most affordable hosting solutions for you.

If I need extra features or a custom design, will that also be free?

No, the basic website is completely free. However, if you need additional features, an e-commerce setup, or custom designs, there will be a charge.

What do I need to do to get a free website?

Just contact us and share your business details. We will create a free, professional website for you.

Will you also help with marketing my website?

Yes, we provide digital marketing, SEO, and social media marketing services, but these are paid services.

What is the process for getting a free website from AKSoftTech?

  1. Contact Us – Share your business details with us.
  2. Design & Development – We will create your free website.
  3. Review & Publish – You review the website, and we make it live.
  4. Marketing (Optional) – You can choose additional marketing services.
